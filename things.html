About Me:

These are a few of my favorite things:

My Computer(s)

I got my first computer in 1989. It was an IBM clone with a dual floppy disk drive (no hard drive) and an amber monochrome monitor. These days I have two laptops, one running Ubuntu and a brand new on with Windows 10.

My Smart Phone

I always said I'd never own a cell phone, but know that I have this smart phone I don't know how I could live without one! I love discovering new apps for the phone and hope to be able to program my own apps one day.

My "Hot Wheels" Truck

I do like the truck, that's why I decided to buy it instead of do a regular lease. I'll be sorry to see it go if I turn it in early - or when I have to sell it.

My Kindle

I love reading, and this kindle makes it possible to carry my whole library with me.

My Little House in Eugene

It's a tiny house in a forgotten neighborhood, but it's quiet and the rent is cheap.

My Flute

I don't play it very much anymore, but it's still one of the things I'd pack first if I was headed out to a deserted island somewhere.
