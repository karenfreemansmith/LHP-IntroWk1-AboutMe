Favorite Hobbies

Writing

I suppose everyone that loves to read wants to grow up to be a writer. I love the magic of storytelling and being transported to other places and times through the written word. I want to make that magic happen too! I'm still an amateur though, with a drawer full of first chapters and half-finished stories.

Computer Graphics

I always liked art, even as a child. Art supplies were expensive though and I never got very good at drawing and painting. Then I discovered "Painter" and found a never-ending supply of art stuff to play with. I also like photography and use Photoshop, Illustrator, GIMP, and Inkscape to create my own graphics.

Programming

I started programming when I was 10 years old and my dad brough home a Commodore PET. (It had a built in monitor, a tiny keyboard, and a cassette tape drive!) I learned BASIC on this machine and eventually graduated to a TRS-80 where I reprogrammed adventure games so my friends could find items they'd really like. These days my interest has evolved into creating interactive websites like the ones I built and maintain at The Practice Test or Aspiring Writers.

Reading

I enjoy reading and have eclectic tastes. I read newspapers, magazines, bathroom walls, classic fiction, bestsellers, and creative non-fiction.

Teaching

One of the best experiences of my life was home schooling my son for 8 years while working as an over-the-road truck driver. Besides that I also enjoyed tutoring other students when I was in school, from 4th grade all the way through college. I've also been involved in training other employees in almost every job I've ever had.

Being Outdoors

One of the best things about trucking is it's like being outdoors all the time, except with air conditioning or heat and a lot less bugs. I've always enjoyed various outdoor activities like hiking, camping, rock climbing, and swimming.
